About Me

Athul Jayaram is a final year student at Amity University pursuing his Dual Degree in Bachelors of Computer Science and Engineering + MBA. He is Google Hall of Fame Recipient. He was the Under Secretary General for IT & Web Operations for Amity MUN, New Delhi. He held many leadership positions such as Microsoft Student Partner (MSP), Mozilla Student Ambassador, CCFIS Student Ambassador, Webmaster of ACM Student Chapter, and the Commander of CyberDome-a Cyber Security Organization of the Government. He organized many conferences including Corporate Meet'16, Confluence'16, ACM to name a few. He won the International Summer Jam'16, a global IoT competition. He also published 6 research papers in International Journal and IEEE.
